Inside Out 2

Director : Kelsey Mann

Genre : Comedy-drama

About the movie

Mann's feature directorial debut was with Inside Out 2, which is about the emotions of adolescence. The film tells the story of Riley's emotions as they find themselves joined by new emotions that want to take over Riley's head. Inside Out 2 aims to capture the hearts of audiences while continuing the legacy of the original film. The film is set to explore the notion that while growing up can be difficult, it's also a journey filled with discovery, connection, and growth.
